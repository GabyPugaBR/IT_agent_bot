# Industry Awareness

## Positioning
Constellations IT Support can be positioned as a lightweight prototype of enterprise AI support systems. It is not trying to match the breadth of a commercial platform, but it demonstrates the same foundational patterns:
- grounded retrieval
- workflow automation
- tool standardization
- multi-agent separation of concerns
- human-support escalation

## Relevant Vendors
### Glean
Glean presents itself as a workplace AI platform with search, assistant, and agents. This is relevant because the Constellations prototype also uses grounded retrieval and agent behavior, but on a much smaller, school-specific scope.

### Moveworks
Moveworks emphasizes enterprise employee support, search plus action, workflow automation, and MCP-ready agent tooling. This aligns strongly with the Constellations pattern of knowledge + workflow + escalation.

### ServiceNow Now Assist
ServiceNow Now Assist represents the enterprise workflow side of AI support. It is a useful comparison point because the capstone app also combines answers with action-taking workflows and human handoff.

## Suggested Presentation Language
"This project is a lightweight, educational prototype inspired by enterprise AI support platforms. Commercial systems such as Glean, Moveworks, and ServiceNow show that modern support tools combine grounded search, workflow execution, and human escalation. Constellations IT Support demonstrates those same ideas in a K-12 IT context."

## Why This Matters
- It shows awareness of current industry direction.
- It helps explain why multi-agent design is useful.
- It positions the project as more than a toy chatbot.
